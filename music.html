My Type of Jams

These four artist or band are my favourite singer, and I often repeated their songs so many times. Their song have filled my playlist in Spotify.





BTS

BTS, an acronym of Bangtan Sonyeondan or “Beyond the Scene,” is a Grammy-nominated South Korean group that has been capturing the hearts of millions of fans globally since its debut in June 2013. The members of BTS are RM, Jin, SUGA, J-hope, Jimin, V, and Jung Kook. There are many reasons that makes me love BTS such as their beautiful lyrics, their incredible dance, moves, and looks and how they take every opportunity to mention their fans "ARMY" in every award they received.


IMSOMNIACKS

Insomniacks is a Malaysian pop band from Kuala Lumpur. Consist of 3 active members which are Iqie, Iskandar and Daniel. The reason I like their songs is because the lyrics is so sweet and they have a very good music. My favourite songs from Imsomniacks are 'Igauan Malam' and 'Pulang'.


KESHI

Keshi, his stage name. The real name of him was Casey Thai Luong, born November 4, 1994. Keshi is an American singer, songwriter, record producer, and multi-instrumentalist. He has accumulated over one billion streams with songs such as "Like I Need U", "Limbo" and "drunk", and my favourite is Limbo.


RED VELVET

Red Velvet is a South Korean girl group formed and managed by SM Entertainment. They originally debuted on August 1, 2014 with the four-member line-up of Irene, Seulgi, Wendy and Joy. A fifth member, Yeri, joined in March 2015. The reason I like this group songs is because they have a great vocals, their concept are unique and they are all stunning.
